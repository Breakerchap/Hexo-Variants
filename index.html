Infinite Hex Tic-Tac-Toe

Connect 6 in a straight line by default. The first move must be at the origin, then every placement must stay within 11 spaces of an existing tile. The starting player opens with 1 move, then normal turns use the current placements-per-turn setting.

Rules & controls
  • Click a board space to place.
  • The first move must be at (0, 0).
  • Later placements must be within 11 spaces of an existing board space.
  • Drag with right mouse button or middle mouse to pan.
  • Scroll to zoom.
  • Winning line length uses the current in-a-row setting.
  • The opening turn is always 1 placement, no matter who starts.
